About us

About us

We Strive to produce fresh, healthy and environment friendly local produce all year round using state of the art technology. Have you ever had freshly picked vegetables? The taste is incredible. We think ours taste even better. We grow our plants in a protected environment and harvest at the peak of freshness. Our precision farming technology allows us to harvest on any specific day compared to conventional farms that can only harvest few times in a year.

Never out of Season

Traditional farms and green houses rely on outside weather conditions. So, they can only have a few harvests in a year with no control on when crops are ready to harvest. We grow crops in a climate controlled facility with precision control and continous monitoring of our plants. So, we can produce superior quality plants, regardless of the weather outside. We can grow plants for you when you need them..

Speciality Crops for Restuarants

Our precision farming technology and methods are a perfect fit to the exacting demands of Restaurants and Gourment Dining. We can customize the crops based on your menu and your needs, and can provide a consistent supply all year round. We work closly with the chefs to cater to their specific needs for fresh vegetables and herbs..

Environment Friendly

We make every effort to be as environment as possible and love our natural resources like Water, Sun and Soil. Our farm uses 90% less water than convetional farming and use Solar Energy to power our farm. We also recycle the nutrients we use, unlike conventional farms that allow fertilizer to seep into the soil and water table..
